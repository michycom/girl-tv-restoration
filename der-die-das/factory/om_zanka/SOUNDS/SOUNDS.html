SOUNDS

 

  

KONIATKO

This is the suite "Alespon 59" recorded by us in Tabor in august `01. Divided into three movements, the first featuring Patrik Kucera and Tomas Cilek, the second featuring Carlos on vocals and the end is Olve Strelow solo with bells overdubbed.

The master tapes of this piece had been wiped, and so we thought it was lost forever. Fortunately, we found out about a cassette copy of the rough mix existing in Berlin. This sound file was made from that tape, hence the sound quality.

OLVE STRELOW SOLO

This is a piece programmed by me on an n-sonic keyboard in january`02. Inspired by Coltrane´s "love surpreme", this is as much techno as I´ll ever get...the guitar synth solo was played by Olaf Gödecke.

OLVE STRELOW SOLO

this was recorded during a performance of "UNIVERSUM Z" in Hamburg, april 2000.

I experimented a lot with old, broken cymbals back then. Most of the times I just turned them around, so that they rested on the bell and played them like that down on the floor.

Soon to be released on the upcoming TIKOZIGALPA compilation. Musicians: Henry Lafrenz-keys, Torben Widdermann-guit, Olve Strelow-bass/drums/perc, Peter Image-bass, Michael Prott-sax
